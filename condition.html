    We currently offer delivery in Taipei within 10km driving distance from our stores. Please make sure to enter the address details correctly, to ensure the order arrives at the correct location. Once this email has been received, we will process your refund and you should see the funds transferred back to the card on which you made payment within 5-7 business days.
